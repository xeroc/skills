# Softonic Platform Guide

## 1. Platform Overview

**Softonic** is a Spanish software download platform and discovery destination founded in Barcelona in 1997. It operates as one of the world's largest software distribution platforms, categorizing, evaluating, and distributing programs for Windows, Android, Mac, and iOS platforms.

- **URL**: https://en.softonic.com
- **Corporate Site**: https://hello.softonic.com
- **Category**: Software Download Platform / Discovery Marketplace
- **Company**: ED Media TIC, Barcelona, Spain  
- **CEO**: Fiona Garvey (as of 2021)
- **Available in**: 18 languages globally

Softonic operates multiple subsidiaries including FileHippo, Appvizer (B2B software recommendations), and Ghacks (tech news). The company has partnerships with major platforms like Download.com and manages ecosystems for other software distribution platforms.

## 3. Who Should Use This

**⚠️ IMPORTANT UPDATE**: As of 2024, Softonic has **permanently shut down their Developer Hub** (self-service submission portal). Most apps now get listed automatically without developer submission.

**Best for:**
- **Established software** with existing user bases (they often add popular software automatically)
- **Windows desktop applications** (their primary focus historically)
- **Security-focused developers** (they have rigorous malware scanning)
- **Global software targeting international markets** (18 language support)
- **Free and freemium software** (their catalog focus)

**Not ideal for:**
- **New/unknown software** seeking initial exposure (self-submission no longer available)
- **SaaS/web applications** (platform focuses on downloadable software)
- **Mobile-only apps** (better served by official app stores)
- **Enterprise-only software** (their audience is consumer-focused)

## 4. Submission Process

**⚠️ CRITICAL CHANGE**: The traditional submission process has been discontinued.

### Current Reality (2024+)
1. **No Self-Service Submission**: The Softonic Developer Hub has been permanently shut down
2. **Automatic Discovery**: They add popular software to their catalog automatically when it gains traction
3. **Partnership Route Only**: Software listing now requires business partnership or advertising agreement

### How to Get Listed Now:
1. **Contact Sales Team**: Email `salesleads@softonic.com` with:
   - Your company website
   - Company location (country specific)
   - Software details and user base

2. **Partnership Inquiry**: They review requests for:
   - Sponsored articles
   - Advertising partnerships
   - Catalog services (minimum €3,000/month commitment)

3. **Wait for Automatic Discovery**: Focus on building user base elsewhere - they may add your software automatically if it becomes popular

### Legacy Process (Discontinued):
- Publishing Center portal (publishing-center.softonic.com) - no longer active
- Self-service app upload and management - discontinued
- Developer Hub management tools - shut down

## 8. Common Mistakes / Gotchas

### Current Era Mistakes:

**1. Assuming Self-Submission Still Works**
- Many developers don't realize the Developer Hub is shut down
- Wasting time trying to access discontinued submission portals

**2. Underestimating Partnership Costs**
- €3,000+ monthly minimum is significant
- Additional costs for sponsored content and enhanced visibility

**3. Wrong Product-Market Fit**
- Trying to list SaaS products on a download platform
- Targeting mobile apps instead of desktop software

### Historical Issues (Still Relevant):

**4. Security Scan Failures**
- Not ensuring clean builds without bundled adware
- Missing digital signatures for Windows software

**5. Content Policy Violations**
- Inappropriate software categories
- Missing or poor quality descriptions

**6. Unrealistic Expectations**
- Expecting immediate traffic without user base
- Not understanding their consumer (vs. enterprise) focus
