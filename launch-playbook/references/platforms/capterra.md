# Capterra Platform Guide

## 1. Platform Overview

**What it is:** Capterra is a leading online software marketplace and review platform that helps businesses find, compare, and select software tools. It serves as an intermediary between software buyers and vendors, offering extensive product listings, verified user reviews, and comparison tools across 700+ software categories.

**Who runs it:** Originally founded as an independent company, Capterra was acquired by Gartner, Inc. However, as of January 2026, G2 (a Chicago-based software insights platform) announced plans to acquire Capterra, Software Advice, and GetApp from Gartner.

**URL:** https://capterra.com

**Category:** B2B Software Marketplace & Review Platform

## 2. Key Stats

- **Domain Rating:** 91 (extremely high authority)
- **Monthly Traffic:** ~3.21M visits per month (US rank #6,950)
- **Search Traffic:** Over 80% of traffic comes from organic search
- **SEO Value:** Estimated $4M worth of organic traffic monthly
- **Reviews:** 2.5+ million verified reviews
- **Products Listed:** 400,000+ software apps across 700+ categories
- **Pricing:** Free basic listings available; premium paid options for enhanced visibility

## 3. Who Should Use This

**Ideal for:**
- B2B SaaS companies of all sizes
- Business software tools and applications
- Companies serving enterprise, SMB, or startup markets
- Software with clear business use cases (project management, CRM, HR, etc.)

**Best fit categories:**
- Project Management Software
- CRM and Sales Tools
- Accounting and Finance Software
- HR and Payroll Systems
- Marketing and Analytics Tools
- Industry-specific business solutions

**Not ideal for:**
- Consumer apps or games
- Hardware-only products
- Services without software components
- Early-stage products without real users

## 4. Submission Process

### Step-by-Step Process:

1. **Visit the Vendor Portal**
   - Go to https://capterra.com/vendors/sign-up
   - Look for "Request a basic listing" or "Submit" button

2. **Create Vendor Account**
   - Sign up with business email
   - Complete company profile information
   - Verify your identity and company details

3. **Choose Listing Type**
   - Free basic listing (limited features)
   - Premium listing (enhanced visibility, multiple site links)

4. **Complete Product Information**
   - Fill out detailed submission form
   - Select appropriate software categories
   - Provide comprehensive product description

5. **Upload Assets**
   - Product screenshots (high quality)
   - Company logo
   - Demo videos (if available)
   - Pricing information

6. **Review and Submit**
   - Double-check all information for accuracy
   - Ensure compliance with Capterra's listing guidelines
   - Submit for editorial review

7. **Editorial Review**
   - Capterra's content team reviews submission
   - May create/update listing using publicly available information
   - Final copy subject to Gartner Digital Markets content team discretion

## 5. Requirements

### Essential Requirements:
- **Live Product:** Working software with active users
- **Business Focus:** Must be business/enterprise software (not consumer apps)
- **Company Information:** Valid business details and contact information
- **Product Description:** Clear, detailed explanation of features and benefits

### Asset Requirements:
- **Logo:** High-resolution company/product logo
- **Screenshots:** Multiple high-quality product screenshots showing key features
- **Pricing Information:** Transparent pricing structure
- **Demo Video:** Optional but recommended product demonstration
- **Feature List:** Comprehensive list of product capabilities
- **Company Description:** Professional company overview

### Quality Standards:
- Products must offer required functionality for their software category
- Information must be accurate and verifiable through public sources
- Professional presentation and legitimate business operation
- Compliance with Capterra's content and listing guidelines

## 6. Strategy & Tips

### Optimization Best Practices:

**1. Category Selection**
- Choose the most specific, relevant categories
- Research competitor listings in your category
- Consider secondary categories for broader reach

**2. Content Optimization**
- Write detailed, keyword-rich product descriptions
- Highlight unique value propositions
- Include specific use cases and business benefits
- Use terminology your target customers search for

**3. Visual Assets**
- Use high-quality, professional screenshots
- Show key features and user interface
- Include multiple views (dashboard, reports, mobile, etc.)
- Ensure images are properly sized and branded

**4. Competitive Positioning**
- Research how competitors present themselves
- Identify gaps in competitor messaging
- Highlight your differentiators clearly
- Position for your target market segment (SMB vs Enterprise)

**5. Review Strategy**
- Encourage satisfied customers to leave reviews
- Respond professionally to all reviews (positive and negative)
- Use feedback to improve product positioning
- Monitor reviews regularly for insights

### Timing Considerations:
- **Best Time to Submit:** When you have real customers and positive feedback
- **Avoid:** Submitting too early in product development
- **Ideal State:** 6+ months post-launch with proven customer satisfaction

## 7. Tips & Tricks from the Community

### Founder Insights:

**Getting Approved Faster:**
- Ensure your website is professional and complete
- Have clear pricing information publicly available
- Include customer testimonials on your site
- Make sure contact information is easily accessible

**Maximizing Visibility:**
- Focus on getting genuine customer reviews quickly
- Engage with the platform beyond just listing submission
- Keep your listing information current and detailed
- Consider premium features for competitive categories

**Content Strategy:**
- Use the exact language your customers use
- Research high-value keywords in your category
- Include specific industry terms and use cases
- Highlight integrations with popular business tools

**Review Management:**
- Set up alerts for new reviews
- Respond within 24-48 hours to all feedback
- Address negative reviews professionally and constructively
- Use positive reviews in other marketing materials

### Common Success Patterns:
- Companies that invest time in detailed, keyword-optimized descriptions see better organic discovery
- Regular engagement (updating listings, responding to reviews) correlates with higher visibility
- Integration mentions (Salesforce, HubSpot, etc.) increase relevance for business buyers

## 8. Common Mistakes / Gotchas

### Submission Mistakes:
- **Incomplete Information:** Rushed submissions with missing details get rejected or deprioritized
- **Wrong Category:** Choosing overly broad or incorrect categories hurts discoverability
- **Poor Quality Assets:** Low-resolution images or unprofessional screenshots
- **Vague Descriptions:** Generic, marketing-speak descriptions that don't clearly explain functionality

### Ongoing Management Issues:
- **Neglecting Reviews:** Not responding to customer feedback damages credibility
- **Outdated Information:** Failing to update pricing, features, or contact details
- **Ignoring Competitors:** Not monitoring how competitors position themselves in same categories
- **Over-Promising:** Making claims in listings that don't match actual product capabilities

### Platform-Specific Gotchas:
- **Editorial Control:** Capterra may edit your listing content - final copy is at their discretion
- **Review Authenticity:** They have strict verification processes - fake reviews get removed
- **Category Competition:** Some categories are extremely competitive; consider niche positioning
- **Traffic Quality:** High search volume doesn't always mean high-intent buyers for your specific solution

### Technical Issues:
- **Website Requirements:** Your website must be accessible and professional for approval
- **Cloudflare Blocking:** Some automated tools may be blocked (affects research but not submission)
- **Contact Verification:** Ensure your listed contact information works and is monitored
