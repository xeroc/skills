# Product Hunt

## Quick Facts
| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| URL | producthunt.com |
| Monthly visits | ~3.2M |
| Backlink | Dofollow, DR 90+ |
| Cost | Free |
| Featured rate | ~10% of submissions |
| Reset | Daily at 12:01 AM PST |
| Best day | Saturday/Sunday (less competition) or Tuesday (most traffic) |
| Relaunch rule | 6 months minimum between submissions |

## Submission Requirements
- Product name + tagline (60 chars max, no hype)
- Description (300-500 words, problem-focused)
- Square thumbnail (240x240px+, high contrast, readable at 40px)
- Gallery: 4-6 images (1270x952px or 2400x1260px, PNG, <500KB each)
- Demo video recommended (30-90 sec, founder narration > polished)
- 3-5 topic tags
- Maker comment ready to post at 12:01 AM

## How to Submit
1. Create account 30+ days before launch (account age matters)
2. Build activity: upvote and comment on other products daily
3. Go to producthunt.com/submit, fill all fields
4. Schedule for 12:01 AM PST on chosen day
5. Check dashboard for yellow "Featured" banner before launch
6. Post maker comment with GIF immediately at launch

## The Featured Gate (Critical)
Only ~10% of products get featured on the homepage. Non-featured products get ~70% less traffic and no newsletter inclusion.

**Featured criteria:** Useful, Novel, High Craft, Creative. Editorial/human decision.

**If not featured within 2 hours of launch: stop intensive promotion.** Driving traffic to an unfeatured listing only benefits PH.

## Self-Hunt vs Hunter
60% of 2024-2025 successes were self-hunted. Hunter follower notifications no longer exist. Self-hunt unless you have a genuine relationship with a superuser (check upvote-bell.com/leaderboard).

## Ranking Algorithm
- **First-hour velocity:** Upvotes in hour 1 weighted ~4x more than later
- **Comment quality:** 1 substantive comment worth ~40-50 upvotes in ranking
- **Account quality:** Established accounts (365+ days, active) carry dramatically more weight
- **Engagement consistency:** Spikes get flagged; steady flow rewarded
- **Hours 0-4:** Products randomly rotated; exact counts hidden

## Optimization
- Build 400+ warm supporters with established PH accounts before launch
- Stagger outreach in 4 waves across the day (don't blast everyone at once)
- First comment: hook + story + 3 benefits + GIF + open question
- Reply to every comment within 5-10 minutes all day
- Generate comment volume (50+ quality comments = strong signal)
- Ask for "feedback" or "support," never "upvotes"

## Hard Rules / Gotchas
- **Vote manipulation detected from 5-6 coordinated accounts** (IP patterns, account age, velocity)
- Public "please upvote" posts on social media = demotion risk
- New/inactive accounts carry near-zero vote weight
- Sudden upvote spikes from new accounts = automatic penalty
- AI wrappers and Chrome extensions face higher featured bar
- PH monitors Twitter/X for coordinated upvote asks

## Post-Launch
- Export upvoter list (PH allows this for makers)
- DM top commenters with personalized thanks
- Cross-post to HN if 100+ upvotes
- Retarget PH referrer traffic ($5/day)
- Publish launch debrief blog post
- Add PH badge (#1/#2/#3 Product of Day) to homepage

## Conversion Reality
- 500-2,000 visitors if featured (not #1)
- 5,000-10,000+ if #1 Product of the Day
- 1-6% signup conversion (varies)
- Paid conversion 2-4x lower than organic search
- PH traffic has worst retention of any acquisition channel
- Optimize for email capture, not immediate conversion

## Pre-Launch Checklist
- [ ] PH account 30+ days old with activity
- [ ] 400+ committed supporters with established accounts
- [ ] All assets ready (thumbnail, 4-6 gallery, optional video)
- [ ] Tagline under 60 chars, plain language
- [ ] Maker comment drafted (300-400 words + GIF)
- [ ] Landing page optimized for email capture with PH-specific CTA
- [ ] Launch scheduled for 12:01 AM PST
- [ ] Team briefed on wave schedule and comment monitoring
- [ ] Response templates prepared for common question types

## Launch Day Checklist
- [ ] 12:01 AM: Go live + post maker comment immediately
- [ ] 12:15 AM: Tweet with PH link
- [ ] Check featured status by hour 2 (abort intensive promo if not featured)
- [ ] Waves: 1 AM (EU/Asia), 7 AM (main list), noon (communities), 6 PM (final push)
- [ ] Reply to every comment within 5-10 min
- [ ] Screenshot ranking for social proof
