# Aura++ Platform Guide

## Platform Overview

Aura++ is an AI-powered product launch platform specifically engineered to solve the visibility problem that indie makers face on larger platforms. Founded by Praneet Brar (@PraneetBrar), the platform revolutionizes product launches through limited daily slots that ensure every project gets maximum visibility rather than being buried among hundreds of other launches.

- **Platform**: Aura++
- **URL**: https://auraplusplus.com
- **Category**: Product Launch Platform / Startup Directory
- **Founder**: Praneet Brar (@PraneetBrar)
- **Founded**: 2025
- **Focus**: AI-powered launches with guaranteed homepage placement
- **Unique Positioning**: Limited to only 18 projects per day (vs 100+ on competitors)
- **Daily Launch Time**: 8:00 AM UTC (consistent across all tiers)

### The Core Problem It Solves

Traditional launch platforms suffer from the "overcrowding effect" where hundreds of products launch daily, making it nearly impossible for startups to gain meaningful visibility. Aura++ solves this by implementing strict daily limits:
- **Free**: 5 slots daily
- **Premium**: 10 slots daily  
- **Premium Plus**: 3 slots daily
- **Total**: 18 projects maximum per day

This scarcity model ensures every project receives genuine attention, homepage placement, and community engagement that would be impossible on larger platforms.
