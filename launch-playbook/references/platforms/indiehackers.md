## How It Works
IH is not a launch platform. It's a community. Direct product promotion fails. Story-driven, value-first content with natural product mentions converts 22x better than announcements.

## How to Launch
1. **Create account,** complete profile (photo, bio, link to product)
2. **Add product page** at indiehackers.com/products (permanent directory listing)
3. **Engage for 2-4 weeks minimum** before posting anything promotional: comment on 3-5 posts/day, answer questions, upvote
4. **First post:** Journey/milestone format, NOT "Show IH: check out my product"
5. **Post format:** Story arc with real numbers, ending with a community question

## Post Format That Converts
```
[Hook with personal struggle]
[The wrong path - what you tried that didn't work]
[The pivot point - what changed]
[Numbered tactics with real data]
[Honest failure admission]
[Community question: "Has anyone else solved X?"]
```

The question at the end is not optional. Posts with direct questions get 3-5x more engagement.

## Title Formula
`[Specific Number] + [Transformation] + [Timeframe]`
- "$0 to 1,200 visitors in 6 weeks"
- "$413/month after 3 failed products"
- Average title: 51 chars (top 50). 66% contain a number.
- 0% of top-50 titles were pure announcements.

## What Works
- **90% value / 10% product mentions** (the proven ratio)
- 15-20 minutes daily engagement is sufficient
- Real revenue numbers (specific dollar amounts)
- Post-mortems with honest failures
- Cross-platform lessons ("How I grew from Reddit" does great on IH)
- Product page updates hit the **daily leaderboard** (free recurring homepage visibility)

## What Fails
- First post = product pitch (credibility death)
- "We launched X today" with no story
- Vague numbers ("making some money")
- Not responding to comments in first 2 hours
- Cross-posting identical PH launch content
- Asking for upvotes

## Ongoing Value
- **Biweekly posts:** Journey updates, lessons, milestones
- **Product page updates:** Each update = one leaderboard entry = homepage visibility
- **Groups:** Join SaaS, Marketing, Landing Page Feedback groups for niche audiences
- **AMA eligibility** at $5K+ MRR
- Optimal post length: 750-1,500 words

## Real Results
- Plausible Analytics: IH was #1 traffic source, credited for early growth to $500K ARR
- One founder: 67 paying customers from IH vs 3 from Product Hunt (same product)
- IH visitors convert at 23% vs PH's 3% (OpenHunts 2024 study)
- 15-20 min/day for 4-6 months builds genuine distribution infrastructure
