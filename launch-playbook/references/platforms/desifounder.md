## 4. Submission Process

**Complete Step-by-Step Process:**

1. **Create Account**
   - Visit: https://desifounder.com/signup
   - Options: Email signup or Google authentication
   - Agree to Privacy Policy, Terms & Conditions, and Code of Conduct

2. **Complete Profile Setup**
   - Add professional headshot and bio
   - Connect social media accounts for credibility
   - Showcase previous projects (automatically displayed from Spotlight submissions)
   - Set notification preferences

3. **Access Submission Page**
   - Navigate to: https://desifounder.com/spotlight/submit
   - Login required to access submission form
   - Review current week's submissions for context

4. **Fill Required Project Details**
   - **Project Name**: Clear, memorable product name
   - **One-liner**: Single sentence tagline describing core value proposition
   - **Description**: Detailed explanation covering:
     - Problem being solved
     - Target audience and use case
     - Key features and benefits
     - Current development stage
   - **Category Selection**: Choose relevant category (SaaS, AI/ML, Productivity, etc.)

5. **Upload Visual Assets**
   - High-quality product screenshots
   - Logo or brand images
   - UI mockups (for pre-launch products)
   - Demo videos (if applicable)

6. **Add Important Links**
   - Official website or landing page (required)
   - App Store/Play Store links (for mobile apps)
   - Social media profiles
   - Community links (Discord, Telegram, etc.)
   - Demo or trial access links

7. **Submit for Manual Review**
   - Click Submit button to enter review queue
   - Submission becomes editable until Monday launch

8. **Manual Review Process**
   - DesiFounder team reviews for quality, relevance, and compliance
   - Checks for originality, accuracy, and community guidelines adherence
   - Approval notification sent via email or platform notification

9. **Monday Spotlight Launch**
   - All approved projects launch simultaneously every Monday
   - Projects become publicly visible and indexed
   - Community voting and engagement begins
   - Content becomes locked (no further edits possible)

10. **Community Engagement Phase**
    - Projects available for community discovery and voting
    - Feedback collection from fellow founders
    - Networking opportunities with voters and commenters
    - Potential for newsletter and social media features

## 8. Common Mistakes / Gotchas

**Submission Mistakes:**

**Content Quality Issues:**
- **Misleading Claims**: Exaggerating capabilities or false metrics (immediate rejection)
- **Vague Descriptions**: Failing to clearly explain product functionality and benefits
- **Poor Visuals**: Blurry screenshots, unprofessional images, or missing visual assets
- **Broken Links**: Non-functional websites or demo links (review failure)
- **Incomplete Profiles**: Submitting with empty or minimal profile information

**Platform Misunderstanding:**
- **Wrong Audience**: Submitting non-startup products to startup-focused community
- **Spam Behavior**: Mass promotional content without genuine community engagement
- **Copy-Paste Content**: Using identical content from other launch platforms
- **Plagiarism**: Stealing content, images, or ideas from other projects

**Timing and Process Errors:**
- **Late Submissions**: Submitting too close to Monday deadline
- **Multiple Submissions**: Attempting to submit same product multiple times within cooldown period
- **Edit Assumptions**: Assuming ability to edit after Monday launch (content locks permanently)
- **Review Expectations**: Expecting immediate approval without manual review process

**Engagement Failures:**

**Community Neglect:**
- **Zero Participation**: Submitting without prior community engagement
- **Ignoring Comments**: Not responding to community feedback and questions
- **Hit-and-Run**: Launching and disappearing without follow-up engagement
- **No Reciprocity**: Not supporting other community projects or discussions

**Follow-up Mistakes:**
- **Single Interaction**: Not leveraging launch for ongoing relationship building
- **Feedback Dismissal**: Ignoring valuable community feedback and suggestions
- **Update Neglect**: Not informing community of product developments and improvements

**Technical and Compliance Issues:**

**Platform-Specific Problems:**
- **Beta Bugs**: Not accounting for potential platform issues during beta phase
- **Mobile Optimization**: Not optimizing content for mobile viewing experience
- **Link Management**: Submitting temporary or redirect links that may break

**Guidelines Violations:**
- **NSFW Content**: Submitting inappropriate content for professional community
- **Intellectual Property**: Using copyrighted images or content without permission
- **Privacy Issues**: Sharing confidential information or trade secrets inappropriately
- **Competitive Intelligence**: Attempting to use platform for competitor research rather than genuine participation
