## Submission Requirements
- Product name and URL
- Short description (listing preview) + long description
- Logo/icon
- Screenshots or demo images
- Demo video (optional, recommended)
- Category tags
- Pricing model
- Optional: discount/deal for Uneed users (earns special badge)

## How to Submit
1. Create account at uneed.best/signup (CAPTCHA required)
2. Go to uneed.best/submit-a-tool
3. Enter product URL (robots auto-collect metadata)
4. Complete remaining fields manually
5. Choose: free queue (weeks-months wait) or $29.99 skip line (choose your date)
6. Add UTM parameters to your product URL for tracking

## Ranking
- Community upvotes during 24-hour launch window
- Top 3 products of the day win badges
- Product of the Week and Product of the Month also awarded
- "Trending" section surfaces popular recent listings

## Optimization
- Offer an exclusive deal/discount for Uneed users (earns a badge, increases engagement)
- Engage with other launches on your day (reciprocity matters)
- Share your Uneed listing link on social/communities to drive votes
- Tag @T_Zahil on X when you launch

## Gotchas
- Free queue can be months-long (has been temporarily closed before)
- 24-hour launch window means you need to drive votes on launch day
- European-skewed audience (founder is EU-based)
- $29.99 paid tier is best value for timing control
