## What It Is
Community marketplace for selling, buying, showcasing, and finding co-founders for side projects. Not a launch platform per se, but the **Showcase** mode gives free visibility + DR 69 backlink.

## Listing Types
| Type | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| For Sale | Actively selling your project |
| Seeking Co-Founder | Looking for a partner |
| Showcase | Visibility, feedback, validation |

## Submission Requirements
- Product name and tagline
- Full description (Markdown supported)
- Product URL
- Screenshots (high quality required, poor quality = rejection)
- Tech stack details
- Monthly revenue range
- Monthly visitors (with proof screenshot option)
- Asking price (if for sale, mark negotiable)
- Items being sold (domain, source code, social accounts, etc.)
- Reason for selling / what a buyer could do next

## How to Submit
1. Create account at sideprojectors.com
2. Click "+ Submit a project"
3. Select listing type (Showcase for pure visibility)
4. Enter product URL (auto-fetches metadata)
5. Complete all fields (auto-saves drafts)
6. Submit for moderation (2-5 business days)

## What Gets Rejected
- Concepts/ideas (must be built and working)
- Templates, reskinned projects
- Someone else's project
- Sports betting, gambling, adult, political content
- Non-English listings
- Poor grammar
- Poor quality images
- Incomplete listings

## Optimization
- Lead with specific numbers in title and first line
- Bullet-point key highlights at top (buyers scan)
- Include concrete metrics: users, MRR, page views, subscribers
- Fill out tech stack completely
- Mark prices as negotiable (increases inquiries)
- Place "For Sale" badge on your homepage (drives cross-traffic)
- Share listing on social immediately after approval (views affect ranking)
