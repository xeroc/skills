## What It Is
Newsletter-first platform. Three products in one:
1. **Launch Directory** - curated product listings
2. **TWITS Newsletter** - weekly email to ~20K founders
3. **Startup Income Reports** - verified revenue database

## Submission Requirements
- Product name and URL (must be live)
- Tagline (100 chars max, "10 words or less" philosophy)
- Logo (square, readable at 50px)
- Featured image (1200x630px, OG-image style)
- Category tags
- Your name/email
- Goal with listing (be specific, not "more traffic")

## How to Submit
1. Go to tally.so/r/npZLWP
2. Fill form completely
3. Jaisal manually reviews all submissions (no SLA, days to weeks)
4. Approved: dedicated page at tinystartups.com/launch/[slug] + potential newsletter feature
5. Top products get "#1 Product of the Week" badge (curator-selected, not vote-based)

## Ranking
**Editorial, not algorithmic.** Jaisal picks winners. No upvote mechanic. Newsletter feature is at his discretion.

## Tagline Formulas
- `[Familiar product] for [new use case]` - "Cursor for slides"
- `[Action verb] + [outcome] + [method]` - "Turn idle internet into real forests"
- Direct result: "Double your ad conversions in minutes"
- Avoid: "powerful," "seamless," "revolutionary," anything over 12 words

## Optimization
- Subscribe to newsletter when submitting (Jaisal notices)
- Tag @ratheejaisal on X when you hit milestones
- Post revenue updates publicly (the Highlights section features founder wins)
- Be specific in "goal with this listing" field (shows you know your audience)

## Gotchas
- No upvote mechanic (editorial curation only)
- No account system (Tally form, no dashboard or edit capability)
- Newsletter feature not guaranteed (editorial discretion)
- Curation creates latency (submit early)
- Low organic directory traffic; value is the newsletter reach + backlink
