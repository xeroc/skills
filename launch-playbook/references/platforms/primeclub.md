# Prime Club Platform Guide

## 1. Platform Overview

Prime Club (primeclub.co) is a members-only lifetime deals marketplace founded by Dev Hisaria in 2022. Unlike typical LTD platforms that feature numerous deals simultaneously, Prime Club specializes in promoting lifetime deals for the "top 7% SaaS companies" with extreme vetting and discretion.

**Category:** Lifetime Deal Marketplace - Premium/Curated
**URL:** https://primeclub.co/
**Founded:** 2022
**Founder:** Dev Hisaria
**Business Model:** Curated, community-driven lifetime deals with focus on quality over quantity

Prime Club positions itself as the antithesis to platforms like AppSumo, emphasizing quality, discretion, and working only with established SaaS companies that already have proven revenue models.

## 4. Submission Process

**Step-by-Step Application:**

1. **Initial Application** (primeclub.co/partner-application-form/)
   - Submit basic company information
   - Provide current ARR figures
   - Describe your product/software
   - Indicate how you heard about Prime Club

2. **Discovery Call**
   - Prime Club team reviews application
   - If approved, they'll schedule a call to discuss:
     - Your revenue model
     - LTD strategy and pricing
     - Timeline and expectations
     - Case studies from similar companies

3. **Due Diligence Process**
   - "Extreme vetting process" mentioned on site
   - Multiple rounds of testing
   - Financial stability verification
   - Product quality assessment
   - Founder background check

4. **Campaign Planning**
   - Develop discrete marketing strategy
   - Create educational content around use cases
   - Plan 30-day promotional campaign
   - Set up tracking and analytics

5. **Launch Preparation**
   - Content creation and review
   - Community preparation
   - Email sequence setup
   - Facebook ad campaign planning

## 6. Strategy & Tips

**Best Practices for Success:**

**Pricing Strategy:**
- Price high enough to be sustainable long-term
- Consider API costs and support overhead
- Build in growth projections for lifetime users
- Don't compete with your regular pricing

**Timing Considerations:**
- Prime Club focuses on 1-2 deals at a time
- Expect 30-day promotional window
- Plan for concentrated launch period
- Coordinate with your regular marketing calendar

**Community Engagement:**
- Participate in Prime Club's Facebook group discussions
- Provide educational content about use cases
- Engage with potential customers during campaign
- Offer excellent support during launch period

**Discretion Benefits:**
- Prime Club's closed community protects from recurring customers seeing deals
- No Google indexing of deal pages
- Targeted promotion only to LTD buyers, not your regular audience
- Helps maintain pricing integrity for recurring customers

## 8. Common Mistakes / Gotchas

**Pricing Pitfalls:**
- Don't underestimate long-term API and support costs
- Avoid pricing so low it's unsustainable
- Consider that lifetime users may be power users with higher resource consumption

**Capacity Planning:**
- Prepare for sudden user influx during 30-day campaign
- Scale support team temporarily
- Ensure servers can handle traffic spike
- Have onboarding systems ready for volume

**Customer Segmentation:**
- Don't expose LTD to existing recurring customers
- Prime Club helps with this, but have your own safeguards
- Prepare different messaging for LTD vs. recurring customers

**Long-term Relationship:**
- LTD users aren't just revenue - they're your community
- Plan for ongoing engagement with lifetime users
- Consider exclusive features or updates for LTD community
- Don't treat them as second-class customers

**Due Diligence Preparation:**
- Have financial documents ready
- Be transparent about business metrics
- Prepare for thorough product testing
- Be honest about technical limitations or API costs
