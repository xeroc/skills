## Quick Facts
| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| URL | fazier.com |
| Total visits | 340,000+ (all-time) |
| Products launched | 4,500+ |
| Newsletter | 2,500+ subscribers |
| Backlink | Dofollow, DR 78+ |
| Cost | Free (with badge requirement) / $79 premium |
| Audience | Founders and marketers (decision-makers) |

## Submission Requirements
- Product name and tagline
- Full description (features, use cases)
- Website URL (live and functional)
- Product screenshots/visuals
- Category/tags
- Founder info

## How to Submit
1. Create account at fazier.com
2. Go to fazier.com/submit

### Free Tier
- Must add Fazier embed badge/backlink on your homepage or footer
- Reviewed within 72 hours
- Dofollow DR 78+ backlink

### Premium ($79)
- No badge requirement on your site
- Pinned at top of homepage for 15 days
- Highlighted across all launches, profiles, and company pages
- Go live instantly or schedule
- Includes Product Hunt launch checklist (valued at $39)

## Optimization
- Pitch to builders and makers, not end consumers (audience is founders/marketers)
- Technical differentiation and workflow improvements resonate
- Less competition per launch day than Product Hunt
- Primary ROI is the DR 78+ SEO backlink (traffic-to-conversion is low)

## Gotchas
- Traffic from Fazier itself is low (third-place products report near-zero referral visits)
- The real value is the permanent dofollow backlink from a high-DR domain
- Free tier requires embedding their badge on your site
- Manually moderated (submissions reviewed daily)
