# LaunchIt Platform Guide

## Platform Overview

**LaunchIt** is a startup discovery and launch platform specifically designed for builders, indie makers, and early-stage startups. Created by indie maker "vwek," the platform focuses on connecting innovative projects with an engaged community of builders, developers, and startup enthusiasts rather than traditional enterprise or investor audiences.

- **URL:** https://www.launchit.site/
- **Twitter:** @launchit__ 
- **Category:** Startup Directory & Launch Platform
- **Founded:** Platform terms effective August 17, 2025 (relatively new platform)
- **Creator:** vwek (indie maker)
- **Target Audience:** Builders, indie makers, early-stage startups
- **Platform Focus:** "Where Builders Launch Projects" - emphasizes the indie/builder community
- **Total Projects:** 348+ startups and tools launched as of March 2026
- **Payment Processing:** Via Dodo Payments

The platform serves as a discovery engine for early-stage projects with a community that actively engages with and tries new products rather than just browsing.

## Submission Process

### Step 1: Account Creation
- **URL:** https://www.launchit.site/register (redirects from /submit)
- **Options:** 
  - Email registration (magic link/OTP)
  - Google OAuth integration
- **Requirements:**
  - Must be at least 13 years old
  - Accurate registration information required
  - Agree to Terms of Service and Privacy Policy

### Step 2: Project Submission
- **Submission URL:** https://www.launchit.site/submit (requires login)
- **Form Fields Required:**
  - Project name and tagline
  - Project description (detailed but concise)
  - Website/product URL (must be functional)
  - Category selection from 8 main categories
  - Logo/icon upload (high-quality required)
  - Product screenshots or demo images
  - Contact information

### Step 3: Asset Requirements
- **Logo/Icon:** 
  - High-quality, professional appearance
  - Square format preferred
  - Clear visibility at small sizes
- **Screenshots:**
  - Multiple product screenshots recommended
  - Show actual product interface
  - High resolution, clear quality
- **Description:**
  - Professional, accurate project description
  - Clear value proposition
  - No misleading information

### Step 4: Review Process
- **Review Type:** Automated reviews with lightweight infrastructure
- **Review Criteria:**
  - Legitimate startup projects and business ideas
  - Compliance with community guidelines
  - Functional product URL required
  - Professional presentation
- **Timeline:** Projects typically go live within hours of submission
- **Rejection Policy:** Full refund for paid plans if project rejected

### Step 5: Launch Day
- Projects appear in "Today" section upon approval
- Immediate visibility to community for voting and feedback
- Categorized placement in relevant sections
- Email confirmation sent to submitter

## Strategy & Tips

### Pre-Launch Strategy:
**Timing Optimization:**
- **Best Launch Days:** Tuesday-Thursday for maximum engagement
- **Optimal Hours:** During US business hours (9 AM - 5 PM EST)
- **Avoid:** Mondays (busy start of week), Fridays (lower engagement)
- **Consider:** Time zones of your primary target audience
- **Holiday Awareness:** Avoid major holidays or industry events

**Community Preparation:**
- **Engage First:** Participate in platform before launching own project
- **Build Relationships:** Upvote and comment on other projects genuinely
- **Study Successful Launches:** Analyze top projects in your category
- **Network Building:** Connect with other builders and founders

### Launch Day Execution:
**Active Engagement Strategy:**
- **Be Available:** Plan to actively monitor and respond to comments
- **Quick Response Time:** Respond to comments within 1-2 hours
- **Authentic Interaction:** Engage genuinely, not just promotional
- **Answer Questions:** Provide helpful, detailed responses
- **Show Appreciation:** Thank commenters and voters personally

**Cross-Platform Amplification:**
- **Social Media:** Share launch across all your social channels
- **Email Marketing:** Notify existing user base about the feature
- **Community Sharing:** Post in relevant online communities
- **Team Coordination:** Have team members engage authentically
- **Influencer Outreach:** Reach out to relevant industry contacts

### Maximizing Platform Features:
**SEO Benefits Strategy:**
- **Free Backlink Opportunity:** Target 20+ upvotes in 48 hours for permanent dofollow backlink
- **Paid Plan Backlinks:** Showcase (5 backlinks) and Spotlight (7 backlinks) plans provide guaranteed links
- **Blog Content:** Spotlight plan includes SEO-focused blog post written by LaunchIt team
- **Long-term Value:** Backlinks remain active indefinitely

**Community Engagement Tactics:**
- **Value-First Approach:** Focus on helping others before promoting
- **Genuine Participation:** Build authentic relationships with other builders
- **Consistent Activity:** Regular engagement beyond just launch day
- **Knowledge Sharing:** Share insights and experiences openly
- **Feedback Implementation:** Actively incorporate community suggestions

### Post-Launch Optimization:
**Traffic Conversion:**
- **Landing Page Optimization:** Ensure your site can handle increased traffic
- **Email Capture:** Set up prominent email signup forms
- **Analytics Tracking:** Use UTM parameters to track LaunchIt traffic
- **Conversion Funnels:** Clear path from visitor to user/customer
- **A/B Testing:** Test different landing page elements during traffic spike

**Relationship Building:**
- **Follow-Up:** Send personal thank you messages to active commenters
- **Networking:** Connect on social media with other founders met
- **Collaboration:** Explore partnership opportunities that arise
- **Community Participation:** Continue engaging with platform regularly
- **Update Sharing:** Post updates about product development

## Common Mistakes / Gotchas

### Submission and Setup Mistakes:

**Technical Issues:**
- **Non-Functional URLs:** Submitting projects with broken or placeholder product links
- **Poor Mobile Experience:** Not testing product on mobile devices before submission
- **Slow Loading Times:** Product takes too long to load, losing engaged visitors
- **Missing SSL:** Submitting HTTP-only URLs instead of secure HTTPS
- **Server Crashes:** Not preparing infrastructure for potential traffic spike

**Asset Quality Problems:**
- **Low-Resolution Images:** Using pixelated or unprofessional logos/screenshots
- **Generic Stock Photos:** Using obvious stock imagery instead of actual product screenshots
- **Incomplete Screenshots:** Showing empty states or placeholder content
- **Wrong File Formats:** Submitting unsupported image formats
- **Copyright Violations:** Using images without proper licensing rights

**Content and Messaging Issues:**
- **Vague Descriptions:** Writing overly technical or unclear value propositions
- **Misleading Claims:** Overstating product capabilities or features
- **Poor Grammar/Spelling:** Unprofessional writing that undermines credibility
- **Missing Context:** Not explaining what problem the product solves
- **Generic Positioning:** Failing to differentiate from existing solutions

### Community Engagement Mistakes:

**Launch Day Failures:**
- **Absent Founder:** Not being available to respond to comments and questions
- **Delayed Responses:** Taking hours or days to reply to engaged users
- **Overly Promotional:** Every response being a sales pitch rather than helpful information
- **Ignoring Feedback:** Dismissing negative feedback or constructive criticism
- **No Follow-through:** Not connecting with interested commenters after launch

**Platform Misuse:**
- **Spam Behavior:** Creating multiple accounts for vote manipulation
- **Fake Engagement:** Using bots or services for artificial upvotes
- **Cross-Promotion Abuse:** Constantly promoting your launch in comments on other projects
- **Guidelines Violations:** Ignoring community rules and platform policies
- **Burn and Churn:** Launching and disappearing without ongoing community participation

### Strategic Planning Mistakes:

**Pre-Launch Preparation:**
- **No Traffic Plan:** Failing to prepare website for increased visitor volume
- **Missing Email Capture:** Not having signup forms ready to capture interested visitors
- **No Analytics Setup:** Failing to track where traffic and conversions come from
- **Poor Timing:** Launching during holidays, industry events, or when unavailable to engage
- **Incomplete Product:** Submitting before product is genuinely ready for users

**Launch Execution:**
- **One-Channel Dependency:** Only promoting on LaunchIt without cross-platform amplification
- **No Team Coordination:** Teammates engaging inauthentically or spamming
- **Overlooking Showcase Options:** Missing paid promotion opportunities that fit budget
- **Poor Landing Page:** Sending traffic to unoptimized or confusing destination page
- **No Conversion Tracking:** Unable to measure actual business impact of launch

**Post-Launch Issues:**
- **Abandoning Community:** Launching and never returning to engage with platform
- **No Relationship Building:** Missing networking and partnership opportunities
- **Ignoring Feedback:** Not incorporating valuable suggestions from community
- **No Documentation:** Failing to document lessons learned for future launches
- **Unrealistic Expectations:** Expecting viral results without authentic community engagement

### Technical and Compliance Gotchas:

**Platform-Specific Issues:**
- **Age Verification:** Submitting from accounts of users under 13 years old
- **Multiple Submissions:** Submitting the same product multiple times with different accounts
- **Incomplete Registration:** Not fully completing profile setup before submission
- **Payment Issues:** Problems with Dodo Payments integration for paid plans
- **Category Misclassification:** Choosing wrong category, reducing discovery in relevant feeds

**Legal and Policy Violations:**
- **Terms Violations:** Not reading or following platform Terms of Service
- **Privacy Compliance:** Launching products without proper privacy policies
- **Intellectual Property:** Using copyrighted content without permission
- **False Identity:** Creating fake founder profiles or company information
- **Content Guidelines:** Submitting adult content, gambling, or other prohibited categories

**SEO and Long-term Issues:**
- **Broken Backlink Opportunities:** Not optimizing for the free 20+ upvote backlink threshold
- **Poor URL Structure:** Using dynamic or temporary URLs that break later
- **No Long-term Strategy:** Treating launch as one-time event instead of relationship building
- **Analytics Gaps:** Not properly attributing conversions and traffic to LaunchIt
- **Missed Relaunch Opportunities:** Not utilizing relaunch features for sustained visibility
