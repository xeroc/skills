## How It Works
PeerPush uses a mutual support model. Builders earn "PeerPush Points" by engaging with other products (upvoting, commenting, following, visiting). Points influence your product's ranking. Helping others directly helps you.

## Submission Requirements
- Product name and tagline
- Full description (benefits-focused)
- Logo (square, high-res)
- Screenshots (2-4 showing product in action)
- Product URL (live and working)
- Category selection
- Optional: discount code for PeerPush community
- Optional: MRR figure (transparency valued)

## How to Submit
1. Create account at peerpush.net
2. Go to peerpush.net/submit
3. Fill submission form
4. Choose: free (join queue, ~1 week wait) or paid (instant approval + promotion)
5. Post a Maker's Comment immediately when live

## Rankings
- Product of the Day / Week / Month
- Based on PeerPush Points + community upvotes
- Hall of Fame for top performers
- Browse views: Top | Live | Recent | Updated | Discounts

## Optimization
- **Build community presence before launching** (1-2 weeks of engaging earns points)
- Add a discount code (products with discounts get dedicated "Discounts" filter visibility)
- Display MRR if you have it (community values transparency)
- Build-in-public framing in your description (share the "why I built this" story)
- Post Maker's Comment with specific feedback questions
- Reply to every comment (algorithm rewards engagement)
- Post milestone updates after launch ("Updated" sort view = ongoing visibility)

## Gotchas
- Traffic doesn't always convert to users (discovery traffic, not purchase-intent)
- Free queue wait is ~1 week (based on documented experience)
- Paid promotion mechanics could be clearer (verify what you get at peerpush.net/pricing)
- Platform is newer (launched August 2025), still building reputation
- Audience is founder-heavy, not buyer-heavy
- 50% affiliate commission program exists
