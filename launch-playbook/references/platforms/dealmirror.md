# DealMirror Platform Guide

## 1. Platform Overview

**What it is:** DealMirror is a lifetime deals marketplace that specializes in selling SaaS tools, software, WordPress themes/plugins, digital templates, and creative resources at heavily discounted prices with lifetime access.

**Who runs it:** Founded in 2018 by Ankit Malviya, Pradeep Dubey, and Dheeraj Singh under Arcus Solution Inc., with teams in the USA, India, and Australia.

**URL:** https://dealmirror.com/

**Category:** Lifetime Deal Marketplace / Digital Product Platform

DealMirror positions itself as a curated platform focused on providing entrepreneurs, designers, developers, and small businesses access to premium tools without recurring subscription fees.

## 4. Submission Process

**Step-by-step submission:**

1. **Initial Contact**
   - Visit footer of dealmirror.com and click "Submit a deal"
   - Access direct form at: https://dealmirror.com/product-deal-request/
   - Fill out vendor registration form with complete product details

2. **Application Review**
   - DealMirror team reviews submission for quality and fit
   - Assessment includes product functionality, market appeal, vendor credibility
   - Response typically within a few business days

3. **Partnership Discussion**
   - If approved, team reaches out to discuss terms
   - Negotiation of revenue split (30-70% range)
   - Agreement on deal duration, pricing, promotional strategy

4. **Contract Execution**
   - Formal vendor agreement signed
   - Legal terms governed by Delaware, USA law
   - Revenue sharing percentages finalized

5. **Asset Submission**
   - Provide all required marketing materials
   - Submit redemption codes/license keys
   - Create product landing page content

6. **Go Live**
   - DealMirror creates product listing and promotional materials
   - Deal launches with minimum 60-day duration
   - Marketing campaign begins across their channels

## 6. Strategy & Tips

**Best practices for success:**

**Pricing Strategy:**
- Price your LTD at 80-90% discount from regular pricing for maximum appeal
- Lower prices significantly increase conversion rates on the platform

- Avoid major competitor launches in your category

## 8. Common Mistakes / Gotchas

**Pricing Restrictions:**
- Cannot offer same or lower pricing on other platforms during deal period and 60-day refund window
- No price increases allowed for first 2 months post-launch
- Price changes after 2 months require 10-day advance notice

**Product Limitations:**
- White-label or scripted tools explicitly rejected
- Must disclose any tool history or existing versions
- Cannot be a simple reskin of existing popular tools

**Technical Requirements:**
- Must have functional code redemption system ready
- Integration with DealMirror checkout required
- Redemption codes must work immediately upon purchase

**Communication Gaps:**
- Some vendors report initial communication delays during busy periods
- Important to maintain responsiveness during deal review process
- Medical emergencies or team issues can cause temporary delays

**Refund Policy:**
- 60-day refund window is non-negotiable
- Must honor all refunds processed by DealMirror
- Deactivation of refunded codes is vendor responsibility

**Revenue Timing:**
- Payments only in first week of each month
- Bank transfers only, no other payment methods
- Currency conversion and fees deducted from vendor share

**Legal Considerations:**
- All disputes governed by Delaware, USA law
- Cannot terminate deals without 15-day advance notice
- Must maintain product availability for lifetime of sold licenses
