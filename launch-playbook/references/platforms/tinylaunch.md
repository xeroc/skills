## Submission Requirements
- Product name (no tagline mixed in)
- Tagline (under 100 chars, ideally under 70)
- Full description (2-4 sentences, benefit-first)
- Product URL (live and working)
- Screenshots (1-2 minimum, polished with device frames)
- Logo (any aspect ratio)
- Category selection
- Twitter/X handle (optional but recommended)

## How to Submit
1. Create account at tinylaunch.com
2. Prepare assets
3. Hit Submit/Launch button on homepage
4. Products enter pending/scheduled state
5. Share pre-launch "Notify Me" URL with audience before window opens
6. Products compete for upvotes during weekly window

## Key Feature: Pre-Launch "Notify Me"
Before your launch goes live, share your pending launch page. Supporters register for email notification when voting opens. This is free amplification.

## Ranking
- Pure upvote-based within weekly window
- Top 3 win: TinyLaunch badge + DR 71 dofollow backlink
- Anti-fraud measures active (fake upvotes invalidated)
- Top launches: 300-460+ votes. Minimum 50-150 for top 3 shot.

## Optimization
- Tagline formula: `[Verb] + [specific action] + [for whom] + [outcome]`
- Use "Notify Me" pre-launch feature (email prompt > tweet they might miss)
- Join TinyLaunch Discord before launching
- Engage with other launches (reciprocity is real)
- Use screenshot framing tools (tinyshots.app, shots.so, screely.com)

## Gotchas
- Traffic is modest (3K-5K/month) - not a traffic driver on its own
- DR 71 dofollow backlink only for top 3 winners
- Relaunches allowed if first attempt underperformed
- Low stakes by design - explicitly encourages first-time launchers
