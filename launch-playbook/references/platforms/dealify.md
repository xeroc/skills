# Dealify Platform Guide

## 1. Platform Overview

**Platform Name:** Dealify  
**URL:** https://dealify.com  
**Founded:** 2018  
**Founder:** Tom van den Heuvel  
**Location:** Eindhoven, Netherlands  
**Category:** Lifetime Deals Platform / SaaS Marketplace  

Dealify is a premium lifetime deals platform specifically focused on growth hackers, marketers, and entrepreneurs. Unlike broader deal platforms, Dealify curates high-quality software tools primarily for marketing, SEO, content creation, web design, and business growth. The platform emphasizes "quality over quantity" and positions itself as serving serious growth professionals rather than general consumers.

**Core Focus Areas:**
- Marketing automation tools
- SEO and analytics software  
- AI-powered business tools
- Productivity and workflow software
- Creative and design tools
- Developer tools and hosting

## 4. Submission Process

**Step-by-Step Application:**

1. **Initial Contact**
   - Visit the "Run a Deal" page at https://dealify.com/pages/run-a-deal
   - Email: support@dealify.com
   - Include basic product information and partnership interest

2. **Product Evaluation**
   - Dealify team reviews your software for quality and fit
   - Assessment of target market alignment with their audience
   - Evaluation of product readiness and vendor credibility

3. **Deal Structure Discussion**
   - Negotiate commission rates and deal terms
   - Choose deal type (lifetime, annual, credit-based, or freebie)
   - Set pricing strategy and discount levels
   - Determine deal duration and launch timing

4. **Campaign Preparation**
   - Dealify team prepares professional deal page
   - Marketing campaign development and A/B testing
   - Asset collection and review process
   - Launch date coordination

5. **Launch & Promotion**
   - Deal goes live with intensive marketing promotion
   - Community engagement and customer support
   - Performance tracking and optimization

**Timeline:** Not specifically disclosed, but case studies suggest preparation takes several weeks

## 6. Strategy & Tips

**Best Practices for Success:**

**Pre-Launch Optimization:**
- Thoroughly test your product before deal launch
- Prepare comprehensive FAQ and support documentation
- Set up dedicated support channels for deal traffic
- Create onboarding sequences for new users
- Plan for increased server load and user volume

**Pricing Strategy:**
- Offer significant discount (typically 85-95% off annual price)
- Structure tiers if your product has multiple plans
- Consider lifetime value vs. immediate revenue needs
- Price competitively with current Dealify offerings

## 8. Common Mistakes / Gotchas

**Technical Pitfalls:**
- **Inadequate stress testing:** Many deals fail due to server overload during launch
- **Poor onboarding experience:** Complex setup processes frustrate new users
- **Missing mobile optimization:** Growth professionals expect mobile-friendly tools
- **Insufficient documentation:** Lack of clear tutorials creates support burden

**Business Mistakes:**
- **Overcommitting on features:** Promising features not yet built or stable
- **Ignoring support preparation:** Underestimating support volume during deal period
- **Poor pricing strategy:** Not researching competitive pricing on platform
- **Inadequate refund planning:** Not preparing for refund processing and policies

**Marketing Missteps:**
- **Generic positioning:** Not tailoring message to growth professional audience
- **Weak value proposition:** Failing to clearly communicate growth/ROI benefits
- **Poor asset quality:** Low-quality screenshots or demo videos
- **Inconsistent branding:** Misaligned visual identity with target market

**Partnership Issues:**
- **Unrealistic expectations:** Expecting immediate massive sales without preparation
- **Poor communication:** Not staying engaged with Dealify team during process
- **Commission disputes:** Not clarifying terms and conditions upfront
- **Long-term support neglect:** Treating deal customers as second-class users
