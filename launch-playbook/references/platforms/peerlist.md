# Peerlist

## Quick Facts
| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| URL | peerlist.io |
| Monthly users | 250,000+ |
| Backlink | Dofollow, DR ~60 |
| Cost | Free |
| Cadence | Weekly launches (Monday) |
| Best for | Dev tools, OSS, professional/portfolio products |

## Submission Requirements
- Product name and tagline
- Full description
- Product URL
- **Personal photo** for profile (not logo)
- Screenshots (fill all available slots)
- Cover image (1200x630px)
- Category and tags
- GitHub link (strongly recommended for dev tools)

## How to Submit
1. Create account at peerlist.io
2. Complete your professional profile (Peerlist doubles as a professional network)
3. Submit product via Launchpad feature
4. Schedule for a specific Monday launch
5. All launches go live Monday and compete for the week

## Ranking
- Community upvotes + engagement during the week
- Product of the Week awarded to top performer
- Peerlist is also a professional network, so your profile credibility matters

## Optimization
- Complete your personal profile thoroughly (work history, skills, projects)
- Link GitHub repos (OSS products perform significantly better)
- Engage with other launches in your weekly batch
- The professional network aspect means connections made here have long-term value
- Share your launch link on LinkedIn (Peerlist audience overlaps with LinkedIn)

## Gotchas
- Profile photo must be personal (not company logo)
- Peerlist is a professional network first, launch platform second
- Dev tools and OSS products are the sweet spot
- Consumer products without a technical angle may underperform
- Free platform, no paid acceleration option
