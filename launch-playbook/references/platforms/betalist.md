# BetaList

## Quick Facts
| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| URL | betalist.com |
| Monthly visitors | 100,000-200,000 |
| Newsletter | 60,000+ subscribers |
| Backlink | Dofollow, DR ~74 |
| Cost | Free / ~$99 priority |
| Conversion rate | 12-25% visitor to email signup |
| Typical signups | 50-500 per feature |

## Submission Requirements
- **Custom domain required** (no vercel.app, netlify.app, herokuapp.com)
- **Custom-designed landing page** (no Bootstrap defaults, no template services)
- **Email capture form** (not "follow us on Twitter")
- One-sentence pitch
- 3-5 screenshots or mockups (1200x750px recommended)
- Product category
- Twitter account (required for BetaList account creation)

## How to Submit
1. Create account at betalist.com (Twitter auth required)
2. Read betalist.com/criteria before submitting
3. Click "Submit Startup," fill all fields
4. Choose free (2-month queue) or priority (~$99, reviewed in days)
5. Priority: full refund if rejected; can schedule feature date if accepted
6. Free: silence = rejection (no notification)

## Two Feature Slots
Each startup gets exactly 2 opportunities:
1. **Pre-launch** (waitlist building)
2. **Launch** (product goes live, minimum few weeks after first)

Don't waste slot 1. Both are valuable.

## What Gets Accepted
- Pre-launch or recently launched tech products
- Clear value prop visible on landing page
- Working email capture
- Custom design (not templates)
- Software or hardware startups

## What Gets Rejected
- Template landing pages (most common rejection reason)
- Free hosting subdomains
- Only a video on the landing page (no text)
- "Follow us on Twitter" instead of email form
- Blogs, newsletters, agencies, online courses
- Products with significant existing press coverage
- Direct links to App Store/Play Store/Kickstarter

## Optimization
- **Landing page screenshot IS your BetaList preview.** Make the hero image striking.
- Ask for email only (every extra field kills conversion)
- Show signup counter: "Join 847 others on the waitlist"
- Add referral mechanic (2-3x signup multiplier)
- Urgency: "First 500 beta testers get lifetime access"
- CTA: "Get Early Access" (best) or "Join the Beta" (good). Never just "Submit."

## Making Trending
Getting into Trending nearly triples signup rate. Driven by:
- Upvotes (asking for upvotes is fine on BetaList, unlike PH)
- Comments and engagement
- Twitter activity around your listing
- Share your BetaList listing URL with network immediately upon going live

## Post-Launch
- Email signups within 24 hours (leads go cold fast)
- 4-email drip: Welcome > Behind the scenes > Survey > Regular updates
- Every email starts with one-sentence product reminder (they signed up for dozens of things)
- BetaList badge on landing page ("Featured on BetaList" = editorial approval signal)
- Submit to 10-15 other directories while momentum is warm
- Listing provides long-tail SEO traffic for months

## Strategic Use
BetaList is Phase 1 of a stacked launch:
1. BetaList builds waitlist (100-500 signups)
2. Use feedback to fix showstoppers and sharpen messaging
3. Collect testimonials from beta users
4. Launch Product Hunt with warm audience instead of cold
