# MicroLaunch

## Quick Facts
| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| URL | microlaunch.net |
| Backlink | Dofollow, DR ~50 |
| Cost | Free / $39-49 premium |
| Cadence | Monthly launch windows |
| Best for | Side projects, MVPs, indie tools |

## Submission Requirements
- Product name and tagline
- Description (problem/solution focused)
- Product URL (live)
- Logo and screenshots
- Category selection

## How to Submit
1. Create account at microlaunch.net
2. Submit product with all required fields
3. Choose free or premium tier
4. Products compete within monthly launch windows

## Free vs Premium
- **Free:** Standard listing in monthly batch
- **Premium ($39-49):** Featured placement, priority positioning, guaranteed visibility

## Optimization
- Monthly cadence means less time pressure than daily platforms
- Engage with other products in your monthly batch
- Share your listing link to drive votes during the window
- Indie/maker community, so authentic presentation wins

## Gotchas
- Smaller audience than Product Hunt or Hacker News
- Monthly window means less urgency but also less repeat visibility
- Traffic is moderate; primary value is backlink + community feedback

## Checklist
- [ ] Account created
- [ ] All fields completed
- [ ] Screenshots showing real product UI
- [ ] Submitted before monthly window opens
- [ ] Plan to engage during the launch month
