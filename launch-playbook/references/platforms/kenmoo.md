## Submission Process

1. **Pre-Contact Research**
   - Join the Facebook group as a regular member

   - Review recent successful launches for format/style

2. **Initial Contact**
   - Email Ken Moo directly: hi@kenmoo.me
   - Subject: "SaaS Partnership Opportunity - [Your Product Name]"
   - Include brief product overview and LTD proposal

3. **Partnership Discussion**
   - Ken will review your product and community fit
   - Discuss promotion options (organic post vs. paid promotion)
   - Negotiate affiliate terms if using platforms like Lemon Squeezy
   - Plan giveaway strategy (typically 2-3 free codes)

4. **Content Preparation**
   - Create group-friendly promotional content
   - Prepare giveaway mechanics
   - Set up affiliate tracking if applicable

5. **Launch Coordination**
   - Schedule launch timing with Ken
   - Prepare for Q&A session during first 48 hours
   - Monitor group for questions and feedback

## Strategy & Tips

**Timing:**
- Avoid major holidays and weekends for launches
- Tuesday-Thursday typically see highest engagement
- Morning EST (9-11 AM) often optimal for posts

**Content Strategy:**
- Lead with community value, not sales pitch
- Share your founder story and building journey
- Offer exclusive group member benefits
- Be transparent about limitations and roadmap

**Engagement Best Practices:**
- Respond to ALL questions within 24 hours
- Be active in comments, not just posting
- Share development updates and wins
- Build relationships with active community members

**Affiliate Strategy:**
- Use Lemon Squeezy or similar for automatic affiliate payments

## Common Mistakes / Gotchas

**Posting Without Permission**
- Never post promotional content without admin approval
- Results in immediate ban from group

**Sales-Heavy Messaging**
- Community values authentic discussion over hard sales pitches
- Focus on education and value, not features

**Poor Q&A Response**
- Slow or inadequate responses to community questions kills momentum
- Have team ready for rapid response during launch window

**Insufficient Giveaway Codes**
- Offering too few free codes appears stingy to active community
- Standard expectation is 2-3 minimum, more for larger launches

**Wrong Product-Market Fit**
- Targeting wrong audience (enterprise vs. SMB)
- Not understanding LTD buyer psychology

**Timing Conflicts**
- Launching during other major promotions
- Conflicting with group events or other featured launches
