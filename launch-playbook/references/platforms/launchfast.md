# LaunchIt Fast Platform Guide

## 1. Platform Overview

LaunchIt Fast (officially LaunchIt.fast) is a startup launch platform and Product Hunt alternative founded by Brian Millot (@brian_millot on Twitter). The platform serves as a directory and launch platform for early-stage startups, tech products, and SaaS tools looking to gain exposure and initial users.

- **URL:** https://launchit.fast
- **Submission URL:** https://launchit.fast/submit
- **Featured Products:** https://launchit.fast/featured
- **Category:** Startup Launch Platform / Product Directory
- **Founded by:** Brian Millot (Twitter: @brian_millot)
- **Position:** Product Hunt alternative focused on startup discovery
- **Official Twitter:** @launchitfast
- **Tagline:** "Launch Faster. Grow Smarter."

The platform positions itself as "The go-to platform for innovators, makers, and early-stage startups ready to put their products in front of an engaged community" with a focus on providing qualified traffic rather than vanity metrics.

## 4. Submission Process

### Complete Step-by-Step Process:

1. **Pre-submission Preparation:**
   - Ensure website is fully functional and professionally designed
   - Prepare high-quality product screenshots (multiple angles/features)
   - Write compelling product description and headline
   - Set up analytics tracking to measure traffic impact

2. **Visit Submission Page:** 
   - Navigate to https://launchit.fast/submit
   - Review the three pricing tiers displayed

3. **Choose Your Launch Plan:**
   
   **Basic Launch (Free):**
   - Free listing with moderate exposure
   - Community exposure and basic homepage placement
   - **Requirement:** Backlink to LaunchIt.fast on your website
   - **Requirement:** Display LaunchIt badge on your site
   - Choose your launch date

   **Premium Launch ($9 - Most Popular):**
   - High Authority DoFollow backlink (permanent SEO value)
   - Long-term traffic and indefinite search visibility
   - Guaranteed exposure: "Hundreds of visitors in week one"
   - Social media promotion on X (@launchitfast)
   - Verified badge on your listing
   - No backlink requirement
   - 25% discount on future premium launches

   **Get Featured ($39/month):**
   - Homepage featured placement (first thing visitors see)
   - Sidebar placement on every tool page sitewide
   - #1 position in relevant category pages
   - 10x more traffic than regular listings
   - Cancel anytime, no lock-in contract

4. **Fill Submission Form:**
   - Product name and tagline (under 60 characters recommended)
   - Product URL
   - Category selection
   - Detailed product description
   - High-quality screenshots/images
   - Contact information
   - Choose specific launch date

5. **Review and Submit:**
   - Double-check all information for accuracy
   - Ensure website is ready for increased traffic
   - Submit form and await team review

6. **Review Process:**
   - **Timeline:** Products reviewed and approved "within hours"
   - **Acceptance Rate:** High for quality products meeting standards
   - **Feedback:** Quick response from responsive team

7. **Go Live:**
   - Product appears on platform on selected launch date
   - Premium launches receive social media promotion same day
   - Begin community engagement immediately

### Payment and Refund Policy:
- **Payment:** One-time fee for Premium Launch ($9)
- **Refund Policy:** Full refund if product not accepted by team
- **No Refunds:** Once product is accepted and added to site
- **Monthly Billing:** Get Featured is recurring monthly subscription

## 6. Strategy & Optimization Tips

### Launch Timing Strategy:

**Optimal Launch Windows:**
- **Best Days:** Monday-Wednesday for maximum exposure throughout the week
- **Peak Engagement:** Monday launch → Wednesday traction peak (confirmed by testimonials)
- **Avoid:** Major holidays, industry conference weeks, end of month
- **Time Zones:** Consider target audience's primary timezone
- **Sequential Strategy:** Plan launches during high-volume months (platform shows seasonal patterns)

### Pre-Launch Preparation (2 weeks out):

**Community Building:**
- Research other products launching same day for networking
- Prepare social media announcement drafts
- Build email list notification sequence
- Set up customer support for increased inquiries
- Stress-test website for traffic spikes

**Content Optimization:**
- A/B test product headlines (keep under 60 characters)
- Prepare multiple screenshot variations showcasing key features
- Write benefit-focused descriptions, not feature lists
- Create FAQ section for common questions
- Develop elevator pitch for community responses

### Launch Day Execution:

**Immediate Actions (First 2 Hours):**
- Monitor platform listing for comments and respond within 15 minutes
- Share launch across personal and company social channels
- Send announcement to email list with direct link
- Engage with other products launching the same day
- Thank early supporters individually

**Throughout the Day:**
- Check traffic analytics every few hours
- Respond to all questions thoughtfully and promptly
- Share progress updates on social media
- Build relationships with other founders in comments
- Monitor website performance and user experience

### Community Engagement Best Practices:

**Response Strategy:**
- **Speed:** Respond to comments within 1 hour during business hours
- **Quality:** Provide detailed, helpful responses to questions
- **Networking:** Engage with other launching founders
- **Value-Add:** Use feedback to demonstrate product improvements
- **Relationship Building:** Turn interactions into long-term connections

**Evidence-Based Engagement ROI:**
- "Three partnership DMs in the first two days" (Marcus Okonkwo, Gridleap)
- "Real upvotes, real comments, real sign-ups" (Ravi Kapoor, Cuecard AI)
- Community feedback leads to product improvements before paid marketing

### Premium vs. Basic Launch Decision Matrix:

**Choose Premium Launch ($9) When:**
- You need SEO backlinks (DoFollow from DR 25 site)
- Social media promotion would be valuable
- You want guaranteed traffic ("hundreds of visitors")
- Professional credibility (verified badge) matters
- You plan future launches (25% discount on next premium)
- You're raising capital (verified status for investor credibility)

**Choose Basic Launch (Free) When:**
- Budget is extremely limited
- You can implement backlink and badge requirements
- You're testing the platform before premium investment
- Your product has built-in viral mechanics
- You have strong social media presence to compensate

**Upgrade to Get Featured ($39/month) When:**
- Traffic quality justifies the investment
- You're seeing strong conversion from initial launch
- You need sustained visibility over months
- Competition in your category is high
- You have budget for ongoing customer acquisition costs

## 8. Common Mistakes & Pitfalls to Avoid

### Pre-Launch Mistakes:

**Website & Product Issues:**
- **Poor Design Quality:** Submitting with unprofessional or broken website design
- **Incomplete Functionality:** Launching with non-functional features or broken links
- **Missing Mobile Optimization:** Not testing mobile experience before launch
- **Slow Loading:** Website that can't handle traffic spikes
- **Missing Contact Information:** No clear way for users to reach support

**Content & Messaging Problems:**
- **Vague Value Propositions:** Generic descriptions that don't explain benefits
- **Poor Screenshots:** Low-quality, unclear, or missing product images
- **Long Headlines:** Exceeding optimal character limits for platform display
- **Feature Lists Instead of Benefits:** Telling what it does vs. why it matters
- **Missing Category Selection:** Not choosing the most relevant product category

### Launch Day Errors:

**Community Engagement Failures:**
- **Slow Response Times:** Taking hours to respond to comments and questions
- **Generic Responses:** Copy-paste answers that don't address specific questions
- **Ignoring Other Launches:** Not engaging with other founders launching same day
- **No Social Amplification:** Forgetting to share launch on social channels
- **Abandoning Early:** Leaving the platform after initial posting

**Technical Oversights:**
- **Analytics Not Set Up:** Missing traffic measurement and conversion tracking
- **Customer Support Unprepared:** No system for handling increased inquiries
- **Payment Processing Issues:** Problems with checkout/subscription systems
- **Email Sequences Not Ready:** Missing follow-up for new users from platform

### Strategic Missteps:

**Pricing Plan Selection Errors:**
- **Basic Launch Overconfidence:** Choosing free option when Premium would deliver better ROI
- **Missing Featured Opportunity:** Not upgrading when traffic quality justifies investment
- **Backlink Compliance Issues:** Basic Launch failure to implement required backlinks
- **No Long-term Planning:** Not considering future launch strategy or discounts

**Post-Launch Problems:**
- **Immediate Exit:** Leaving platform immediately after launch day
- **No Follow-up:** Missing opportunities to build on initial traction
- **Ignoring Feedback:** Not implementing community suggestions for product improvements
- **No Relationship Building:** Treating platform as one-time transaction vs. community

### Platform-Specific Gotchas:

**Submission Issues:**
- **Rejection After Payment:** Platform offers full refund if not accepted, but delays launch
- **Duplicate Detection:** Platform identifies and rejects repeat submissions
- **NSFW Content Auto-Rejection:** Any adult content triggers immediate rejection
- **Custom GPT Restrictions:** Most AI chat tools without substantial platforms rejected

**Featured Placement Misunderstanding:**
- **Monthly Recurring Confusion:** Get Featured is subscription, not one-time
- **Cancellation Process:** Must actively cancel to stop monthly charges
- **ROI Expectations:** Featured works best with products already showing good conversion

### Quality Standards Reality Check:

**What Gets Rejected:**
- Websites that look like basic WordPress templates
- Products with no clear use case or target market
- Broken or incomplete feature sets
- Sites with no privacy policy or terms of service
- Products that are just content sites without tools/functionality

**Red Flags the Team Watches For:**
- Mass submission across multiple similar products
- Generic or AI-generated product descriptions
- No social proof or credibility indicators
- Unrealistic claims about product capabilities
- Missing business contact information
